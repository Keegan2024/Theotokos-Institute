About Theotokos Institute

Theotokos Institute, founded in 2010, is a leading center for interdisciplinary research and education. Our mission is to advance knowledge in theology, social sciences, and sustainable development through collaborative initiatives and community engagement.

Our Vision

We aim to bridge academic research with practical applications, fostering innovation and ethical leadership in a rapidly changing world.

Our Team

Our faculty and researchers are experts in their fields, dedicated to mentoring the next generation of scholars and professionals.
