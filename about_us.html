About Theotokos Institute

Founded in 2010, Theotokos Institute is a faith-based college dedicated to academic excellence and community service. We offer interdisciplinary programs in theology, social sciences, and liberal arts.

Mission

To educate and inspire students to become ethical leaders and global citizens through a holistic curriculum.

History

Established to promote faith-based education, we have grown into a vibrant academic community with a focus on innovation and inclusivity.
